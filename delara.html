Biography

Delara Soltani is a researcher passionate in complex fluid dynamics, with a particular focus on pulsatile liquid jets and micro synthetic jets. Her current research investigates the application of synthetic jets in microfluidic devices, where these unique jets generate momentum flux while operating under zero net mass flux conditions.
